How to Install Bookmarklets:

  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B in most browsers)
  2. Drag any button below to your bookmarks bar
  3. Click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar when you want to use it

Important: Don't click the buttons below! Drag them to your bookmarks bar.

Amazon Trump Tarrif Calculator

On any Amazon product page, this bookmarklet will show how much of the price comes from Trump's tarrifs. It pulls the current tarrif rate from https://www.tradecomplianceresourcehub.com/2025/04/29/trump-2-0-tariff-tracker/ using r.jina.ai and displays the calculation at the top of the product page.
